Fiber Broadband

Enjoy the Fastest Fiber Broadband Internet in Rwanda

Flex Faiba is high-speed internet connectivity offered through fiber optic infrastructure. It is provisioned through last-mile fiber access to the premises through BSC's end-to-end fiber installations. It involves physical assessment from BSC’s nearest Point of Presence to the customer premises, where it is terminated, tested, and commissioned. BSC Enterprise packages range from any number of Mbps to Gbps, depending on customer needs.

Benefits

  • High-speed internet
  • Internet Backup services
  • Customer Relationship management
  • VPN capacity
  • Quicker Troubleshooting
