Fixed Wireless Broadband

Get Fast Internet with our Wireless Broadband

This is a type of high-speed Internet access where connections to customers use radio signals rather than cables. BSC’s terrestrial wireless network is deployed country-wide, and customers can be directly connected to this network by either Point to Point (P2P) or Point to Multipoint (PTMP).
